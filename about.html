About Me

My name is Ethan Zhao and I am a second year student at Georgia Institute of Technology. I’m studying the intersection of computer science and design through a Computational Media major with a concentration in media and interaction design. I’m currently interested in learning about UI/UX design in video games.

I also am passionate about promoting diversity and inclusion, especially of queer folks working within STEM fields.

In my free time, you may find me making art, playing my violin or playing some video games with friends.
