Paper Planes

VGDev Project
Year: Spring 2022
Type: Game
Role: UI / Art
 

Title splash for Paper Planes game.

In Spring 2022, I joined the Georgia Tech organization VGDev. This was my first time attempting video game development. During my time on the Paper Planes team, I learned a lot! For this game, I primarily worked on the ui and art. I designed the title splash you see above as well as some other textbox/text container assets.

Paper Planes is one of the games being developed under the Georgia Tech student organization VGDev in the Spring of 2022. It plays as a 2D side-scroller platformer with unique movement mechanics and resource management. With a focus on human nature, self-love, and discovery, its whimsical story shines through. The player plays as a Wind Goddess, and uses the power of their wind to control a paper plane and guide it through different levels. Throughout the game there are levels with enemies and obstacles that will block the plane, and the goal is to clear the levels and progress through the story to send this paper plane to its intended recepient and deliver an important message.
